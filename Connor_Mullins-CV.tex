\section*{RESEARCH EXPERIENCE}

\resumeSubheading
{McLellan Lab, UT Austin}
{Sept 2024 -- Present}
{Undergraduate Researcher}
{}

\begin{itemize}
\item Engineer stabilized HCMV Pentamer and Trimer glycoprotein complexes for structure-based vaccine design using structure-guided and computational approaches (ThermoMPNN, ProteinMPNN); stabilizing substitutions include proline substitutions, disulfide engineering, and cavity-filling mutations; characterize constructs by BLI, SPR, DSF, and negative stain EM.
\item Independently collect and process cryo-EM datasets; determined structures of KSHV gH/gL in complex with Fabs to 3.8 \AA\ and HCMV Pentamer with Fabs to 3.6 \AA\ using cryoSPARC.
\item Performed AlphaFold-based computational screening of antibody-antigen interactions across MPXV envelope-expressed antigens, identifying OPG153 as a target of broadly neutralizing antibodies; work contributed to publication in \textit{Science Translational Medicine} (2025).
\item Developing a novel strategy to determine cryo-EM structures of small proteins ($<$30 kDa) via covalent nanobody dimerization, exploiting C2 symmetry to overcome preferred orientation and particle size limitations.
\end{itemize}

\resumeSubheading
{Westover Lab, UT Southwestern}
{May 2024 -- Aug 2024}
{Research Intern}
{}

\begin{itemize}
\item Crystallized KRAS G12D and KRAS G12D/R68S double-mutant variants; solved X-ray crystal structures to characterize structural mechanisms underlying clinical drug resistance in pancreatic and colorectal cancer.
\item Analyzed genome-wide CRISPR dependency data (DepMap) across KRAS alleles in colorectal cancer cell lines; identified allele-specific vulnerabilities including ribosome biogenesis pathway enrichment in G12D lines (GSEA NES $= -1.86$, FDR $< 0.001$), consistent with G12D-preferential PI3K/AKT/mTOR signaling.
\textit{[\href{https://github.com/conmmul/kras-depmap}{github.com/conmmul/kras-depmap}]}
\item Expressed and purified a panel of novel KRAS drug-resistance variants generated by lentiviral insertional mutagenesis for biochemical and structural follow-up studies.
\end{itemize}
